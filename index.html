I'm Mariam, a passionate Frontend Developer dedicated to crafting simple and engaging user experiences. My journey in web development began out of curiosity and a love for learning, and quickly turned into a deep passion for building elegant, functional interfaces that reflect the identity of each project and meet user needs.

My Services

Responsive & Professional Frontend Development

Turning Ideas & Designs into Interactive Experiences

I bring static designs or rough concepts to life by building interactive, engaging, and visually dynamic web pages — enriched with smooth animations and intuitive interactions.

Website Setup & Deployment

I help prepare and launch websites on platforms like GitHub Pages and Netlify, ensuring they're fully functional, accessible online, and ready for real-world use.
